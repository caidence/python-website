What I enjoy more than anything is making a meaningful impact on the lives of other people. That is what inspired me to put myself out in the world and try to make a difference through this website. A little about myself

Hello everyone! My name is Caiden, but most people call me Caid, Cai, or Caidence. I am currently a computer science student at Wichita State University, and hope to graduate by 2022! I have an associate degree in Information Technology Systems from WSU Tech. I am an intern at NetApp where I spend most of my working hours writing Bash and Python programs. NetApp is a company that offers cloud services and storage solutions. My hobbies consist of hanging out with friends and family, playing the piano, and obviously programming! Out of all my hobbies, hanging out with friends and family is by far my favorite; but even though it is my favorite, the hobby I spend the most time on is programming. I love programming and coding, so much so that I wrote all of the code for this website which is about how to code in Python! I am not sure where I want to go with my career yet, but I have a few different directions that I'm looking into. That is a little background information about me.
