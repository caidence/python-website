Programming

The goal of this website is not only to teach you Python, but to teach core programming concepts. Much of what you learn here will not only apply to Python, but to most other programming languages too. Python was chosen because it is one of the easiest languages for beginners to get started with.

Design

Through this website's tutorials you will learn how to design your programs using different paradigms such as functional programming and object oriented (OO). It is important to understand the pro's and con's of each, and when you should apply which paradigm depending on your specific needs.

Algorithms

They may sound scary, but I assure you that they're not! Algorithms are a part of every programmer's life, and an important part of programming. Many companies hire candidates based on their ability to design, create, and solve algorithms. Everyone who calls themselves a programmer should at least know the basics of algorithms.
